<p>FileFlow scans your files periodically and organizes them according to your rules.</p><p><strong>Features:</strong><ul><li>Rules: Use regex to precisely target files.</li><li>Actions: Choose what to do with the files - copy, move, rename, or delete.</li><li>History: Recent executions are stored (locally).</li><li>Private: Fully offline; your data never leaves your device.</li><li>Lightweight: Runs in the background with minimal battery and memory usage.</li></ul></p>
